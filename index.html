About

25+ years in defense & aerospace

Paul Stewart is a Senior Principal Engineer at L3Harris Technologies and founder of Blue Nebula Group. With over 25 years in the defense and aerospace industry — including service in the U.S. Air Force and leadership roles at AEVEX Aerospace, ForceX, and ZMicro — Paul brings deep expertise in ISR systems, government contracts, and advanced technology integration.

His career spans engineering, program management, and executive leadership, giving him a rare ability to bridge the gap between technical complexity and business outcomes. He holds an MBA from the University of Phoenix and a BS in Information Technology.

Blue Nebula Group was founded to bring practical, no-nonsense AI solutions to the industries Paul knows best: defense, aerospace, and other technical fields where precision and reliability aren't optional.
